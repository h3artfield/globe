The African Union stated that its Bureau of the Assembly of Heads of State and Government met on 21 July 2020 to review progress in trilateral negotiations on the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam involving Egypt, Ethiopia, and Sudan.

The communique describes the issues under negotiation as the first filling and annual operation of the GERD and future development projects on the Blue Nile River upstream of the GERD.

The AU Bureau said the parties should work toward a binding agreement on filling and operation of the GERD and a broader comprehensive agreement on future Blue Nile developments.
